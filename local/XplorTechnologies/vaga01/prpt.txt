Job Description

As the VP of SaaS Engineering for Momence (Xplor), you play a crucial role in leading and managing the Momence engineering and product department. Your responsibilities involve overseeing multiple teams and you will lead the evolution of engineering practices toward agentic, AI-enabled delivery models, enabling teams to responsibly design, build, and ship AI-driven features that accelerate product innovation and customer value.

Developing & Designing


Analyze complex application and platform landscapes across SaaS, integrations, and ecosystem services

Controlling & Managing


Review vendor proposals and oversee in-flight technical work to ensure quality, scalability, and value realization

Continuously optimize engineering and delivery practices, including modern, AI-enabled and agentic workflows 
Building Community


Advise and mentor internal leaders and teams on core technical concepts, frameworks, and emerging practices 
Build and leverage a strong network across the Xplor engineering community to share knowledge and accelerate outcomes
